DIthesis

DIthesis is a LaTex2e document class for preparing B.Sc. theses according to the requirements of the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications at the University of Athens as of September 1, 2011.

You can download it here. It contains two sample TEXs that you can use as guidelines for your thesis ( with Arial | without Arial ), two documentation PDFs ( with Arial | without Arial ), a picture of Athena, and the class itself.

In addition, you can download an OpenOffice template for the required CD cover here (made by Pavlos Kollias).
